    The seam interface #

    Seam bundles what F must supply to the headline assembly: a well-order lt of ℕ, an X-free ℒₒᵣ-formula φ defining it, and the order-type lower bound ε₀ ≤ ‖lt‖. The two definability obligations are discharged here (hprec/hprecXPos, via the lemmas above); the lone remaining obligation is ge — the ε₀-completeness girder (mathlib-only; see PENDING_WORK.md). The seam needs only ε₀ ≤ orderType lt (NOT =): the contradiction is ‖lt‖ ≤ 2^β, β < ε₀.
