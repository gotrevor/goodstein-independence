### ⭐ The research correction: `zFresh` under `zsubst`/`red` is DIRECTIONAL, not an equality
Lap 126's baton modelled `zFresh_zsubst` on `zReg_zsubst` (an *equality* `zReg (zsubst d a t) = zReg d`).

invariant is the **implication** `ZFresh d → ZFresh (zsubst d a (numeral n))` (downward-closed), NOT an
equality. Every downstream lemma (`ZFresh_red`) is therefore an implication too.
