4 Route B — Wainer growth rates (mainline)

The route adopted after the 2026-07-01 route decision. It proves non-provability from rates of growth and, unlike Route A, needs no internalized cut-elimination and no Gödel-II wiring: if \(\mathsf{PA}\) proved \(\gamma \), Wainer would bound the Goodstein length function below one fixed \(f_o\) with \(o{\lt}\varepsilon _0\); but the Cichoń/Caicedo lower bound (already machine-checked) shows no fixed \(f_o\) can bound it. The lower-bound half is done and clean; the single remaining gap is the Wainer classification (Def 3.4), surfaced here as one named axiom.
